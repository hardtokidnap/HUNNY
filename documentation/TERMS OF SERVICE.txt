HUNNY - TERMS OF SERVICE

Last updated: 2026-06-12
Repository: https://github.com/hardtokidnap/HUNNY


1. WHAT THESE TERMS COVER

HUNNY is a Discord moderation bot. It watches one designated "honeypot"
channel per server and automatically bans any non-staff member who posts
there, deleting their messages.

These terms apply to the public instance of the bot operated by the
repository maintainer (hardtokidnap, "the Operator"). If you self-host the
bot from this repository, you become the Operator of your own instance and
these terms apply to your instance with you in that role.


2. ACCEPTANCE

By adding the bot to a Discord server, or by keeping it in a server you
administer, you agree to these terms on behalf of that server.

Regular members do not need to accept anything: the honeypot channel is
marked by a pinned warning notice, and posting in that channel after the
notice is pinned is treated as triggering the trap.


3. THE SERVICE

- Server staff designate one text channel as the honeypot using /setup
  (restricted to the server owner and Administrators).
- The setup user posts one message in that channel. The bot pins it as the
  permanent warning notice. From that point the honeypot is active.
- Any non-exempt member who posts in the active honeypot channel is banned
  and their messages from the last 7 days are deleted server-wide.
- Exempt: bots, the server owner, members with the Administrator
  permission, and the pinned notice itself.

The bot acts with the permissions your server granted it when it was
invited. Every ban is executed under your server's own authority.


4. YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES AS SERVER STAFF

- Keep the pinned warning notice in place so members are informed.
- Keep the bot's role positioned correctly. The bot cannot ban anyone
  whose highest role is at or above its own; those bans are skipped and
  logged, not retried.
- Moderation outcomes are yours. The bot is a tool that executes the rule
  you configured; reviewing and reversing bans (Server Settings, Bans) is
  your job and entirely possible at any time.
- Do not use the bot to trap or harass specific people, and do not use it
  in any way that violates Discord's Terms of Service or Community
  Guidelines.


5. AVAILABILITY AND TERMINATION

The service is provided free of charge with no uptime guarantee. The
Operator may remove the bot from any server or shut down the instance at
any time. You may remove the bot from your server at any time; doing so
deletes your server's stored configuration automatically (see the Privacy
Policy).
